Lenovo Wireless Mouse N50 is a three-button wireless IR optical mouse with a nano dongle making you to enjoy the free control without the disordered cables and it's no need to remove the dongle when moving the system. The operation range is above 10 meters in order to maximize your freedom. 1AA battery life up to 1 year. With cool black-diamond ID and middle size.- 2.4G wireless mouse - middle size dimensions(nearly full size) - As far as 10 meters control distance - 1000 dpi optical solution - 1year battery life
